Here the best Animes ever

One piece you'r uncel

One Piece is a Japanese manga and anime about Monkey D. Luffy, a pirate with a rubber body, and his crew seeking the legendary treasure "One Piece" to become Pirate King. It's a story of adventure, friendship, and dreams.

naruto

A Japanese anime and manga following Naruto Uzumaki, a ninja with dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader of his village. It explores themes of friendship, perseverance, and overcoming loneliness.

promised neverland

A suspenseful anime and manga about a group of orphans who discover their idyllic home is a front for a sinister purpose. They plan their escape while uncovering dark secrets about their world.

parasyte

A Japanese anime and manga about Shinichi Izumi, a high schooler infected by a parasitic alien that takes over his right hand. Together, they fight other parasites while exploring themes of humanity and survival.

noblesse

A Korean webtoon about Cadis Etrama Di Raizel (Rai), a noble vampire who awakens after 820 years. With his loyal servant Frankenstein, he protects humanity while uncovering secrets about his past.

ninja kamui

A Japanese manga about Kafka Hibino, a man who gains the ability to transform into a kaiju (monster). He uses his new powers to protect humanity from monstrous threats while hiding his secret.

mashle

A fantasy manga about Mash Burnedead, a boy without magic in a magic-dominated world. With incredible physical strength, he challenges the system in a magical academy to prove himself.

jujutsu kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is an enthralling anime and manga series by Gege Akutami that revolves around curses and malevolent spirits born from human fears and negative emotions. The story centers on Yuji Itadori, a high school student who finds himself unwillingly drawn into a battle against these curses.

hanter x hanter

A Japanese anime and manga following Gon Freecss, a young boy who becomes a Hunter to find his father. It’s known for its complex characters, moral dilemmas, and strategic battles.

dororo

A Japanese anime and manga about Hyakkimaru, a boy whose body parts were sacrificed to demons by his father. Together with an orphan thief named Dororo, he embarks on a journey to reclaim his stolen body and humanity.

demon sayler

Demon Slayer is a story set in early 20th century Taishō-era Japan, where man-eating demons roam the land each night1. The story follows 13-year-old Tanjiro Kamado, whose entire family is murdered save for his sister Nezuko, who has turned into a demon

charlotte

An anime about teenagers with superpowers caused by a mysterious comet called "Charlotte." Yuu Otosaka, a boy with the ability to possess others, is forced to join a group helping others with dangerous powers.
